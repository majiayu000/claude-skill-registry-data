# Scale Awareness

Use this skill to pick safe strategies based on scale.

## Workflow

1) Estimate task scope and risk.
2) Choose tool-first vs. generate-first approach.
3) Escalate to human approval when needed.
4) Report strategy choice.

## Output expectations

- Provide strategy selection and rationale.
