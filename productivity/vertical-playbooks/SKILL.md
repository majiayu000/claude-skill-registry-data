## Vertical Selection Guide

### Which Vertical to Target?

| Signal | Best Vertical |
|--------|---------------|
| Shopify/WooCommerce mentioned | E-commerce |
| "Billable hours" or "clients" | Professional Services |
| "Deliveries" or "shipments" | Logistics |
| "Contracts" or "compliance" | Legal/Finance |
| "Export", "foreign buyers" | Export Companies |
| "Import", "suppliers overseas" | Import Companies |
| "FTZ", "triangular", "transit trade" | Re-Exporters |
| "Production", "factory", "plant" | Manufacturers |
| "Wholesale", "resell", "SKUs" | Distributors |
| "Job site", "subcontractors", "GC" | Construction |
| "Owner's rep", "CM", "program" | Construction Management |
| "Customs broker", "freight forwarder" | Global Trade Services |

### Quick Qualification by Vertical

| Vertical | Minimum Size | Sweet Spot | Red Flag |
|----------|--------------|------------|----------|
| E-commerce | 100+ orders/mo | 500-5000 orders/mo | <50 orders |
| Professional Services | 10+ clients | 50-500 clients | Solo practitioner |
| Logistics | 20+ deliveries/day | 100-1000/day | <10/day |
| Legal/Finance | 5+ team | 10-100 team | Solo |

## Vertical Discovery Templates

### E-commerce Opener
"I help online stores stop missing sales from inventory issues and slow shipping updates. Mind if I ask a few questions about how you handle orders?"

### Professional Services Opener
"I help service businesses capture more leads and get paid faster by automating the repetitive stuff. Can you walk me through how new clients typically find you?"

### Logistics Opener
"I help delivery companies reduce 'where's my package' calls by 80% with proactive updates. How do your customers currently track their deliveries?"

### Legal/Finance Opener
"I help firms eliminate deadline anxiety and payment chasing with automated workflows. What's your biggest time sink right now?"

## Vertical Objection Handling

### E-commerce
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We have Shopify apps" | "Those handle one thing at a time. We connect everything so nothing falls through." |
| "Too small" | "That's exactly when to automate - before you scale and it gets messier." |

### Professional Services
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We use [software]" | "Great, we'll make it work harder for you by connecting it to everything else." |
| "Our processes are unique" | "That's why we custom-build. One size fits all doesn't work." |

### Logistics
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We have a TMS" | "Perfect - we'll add the customer communication layer it's missing." |
| "Our drivers won't use it" | "They won't have to - it's all automatic based on existing GPS data." |

### Legal/Finance
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Compliance concerns" | "Everything stays in your systems. We just automate the routing and alerts." |
| "Client data sensitivity" | "We don't store any data - it flows through encrypted, never stored." |

## Quick Win Templates

### E-commerce Quick Wins
1. **Order Notification** - Slack/email when order placed
2. **Low Stock Alert** - Warn before stockout
3. **Review Request** - Ask for review 7 days post-delivery
4. **Abandoned Cart** - Email reminder with cart contents

### Professional Services Quick Wins
1. **New Lead Alert** - Instant notification
2. **Appointment Reminder** - SMS/email before meetings
3. **Invoice Sent Alert** - Know when they open it
4. **Payment Received** - Celebrate closed invoices

### Logistics Quick Wins
1. **Dispatch Confirmation** - Customer knows driver assigned
2. **ETA Update** - Automatic when driver nearby
3. **Delivery Proof** - Photo/signature capture
4. **Failed Delivery** - Instant reschedule options

### Legal/Finance Quick Wins
1. **Document Upload Alert** - Know when client sends files
2. **Deadline Reminder** - 7/3/1 day warnings
3. **Signature Request** - Track who hasn't signed
4. **Payment Overdue** - Auto-reminder sequence
