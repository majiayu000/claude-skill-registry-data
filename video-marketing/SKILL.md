```yaml
youtube_specs:
  long_form:
    ideal_length: "8-15 minutes"

    max_length: "60 seconds"
    format: "9:16 vertical"
    resolution: "1080x1920"

  thumbnails:
    size: "1280x720 pixels"
    format: "JPG, PNG, GIF"
    max_file_size: "2MB"
    best_practices:
      - High contrast colors
      - Readable text (3-5 words max)
      - Expressive faces
      - Brand consistency

  optimization:
    title:
      - 60 characters max (visible)
      - Front-load keywords
      - Include numbers when relevant
      - Create curiosity

    description:
      - First 150 characters most important
      - Include target keywords naturally
      - Add timestamps for chapters
      - Links and CTAs
      - Related video suggestions

    tags:
      - Primary keyword first
      - Mix of broad and specific
      - 8-15 tags recommended
      - Include misspellings
```

## Video SEO

### YouTube SEO Strategy

```yaml
youtube_seo:
  keyword_research:
    tools:
      - YouTube Search suggest
      - TubeBuddy
      - vidIQ
      - Google Trends
      - Ahrefs YouTube

    process:
      1: "Identify seed keywords from your niche"
      2: "Use autocomplete for long-tail variations"
      3: "Analyze competitor video keywords"
      4: "Check search volume and competition"
      5: "Map keywords to content ideas"

  on_video_optimization:
    title:
      - Primary keyword near beginning
      - Compelling hook
      - 50-60 characters
      - Avoid clickbait

    description:
      - First 150 chars = mini summary with keyword
      - Full description 200+ words
      - Natural keyword usage
      - Timestamps for chapters
      - Related links

    tags:
      - Primary keyword first
      - Variations and synonyms
      - Broad category tags
      - Competitor tags (ethical)

    closed_captions:
      - Always upload accurate SRT
      - Include keywords naturally
      - Improves accessibility and SEO

  engagement_signals:
    - Watch time (most important)
    - Click-through rate (CTR)
    - Likes, comments, shares
    - Subscribers from video
    - End screen clicks
```

## Лучшие практики

1. **Hook в первые 3 секунды** — захвати внимание сразу или потеряешь зрителя
2. **Оптимизируй для каждой платформы** — не просто репостить, а адаптировать
3. **Добавляй субтитры** — 85% видео смотрят без звука
4. **Используй thumbnails с лицами** — они получают больше кликов
5. **Постоянство важнее частоты** — лучше 1 видео в неделю стабильно
6. **Анализируй retention** — оптимизируй моменты, где зрители уходят
7. **Repurpose контент** — одно видео → несколько форматов
8. **Тестируй thumbnails и заголовки** — A/B тестирование для CTR
