# Trust Research

Use this skill to produce evidence-grounded research outputs.

## Workflow

1) Retrieve sources and extract claims.
2) Validate each claim against its citation.
3) Run consistency checks across summaries.
4) Produce a confidence score and flag gaps.

## Output expectations

- Provide citations for all claims.
- Include a confidence assessment and unresolved questions.
