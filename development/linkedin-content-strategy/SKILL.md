## The Commenting Strategy

Commenting often produces better results than posting alone.

### Why It Works

- Comments on your posts boost engagement by 30%
- Posts with comments get 60% more views
- 10-20 comments/day can increase profile views by 50%
- Meaningful comment exchanges lead to 52% reply rate on follow-up messages (vs 18% cold)

### Commenting Best Practices

**Do:**
- Write 10+ words (thoughtful > generic)
- Add value: insights, examples, data
- Ask follow-up questions
- Comment on posts from accounts with 50K+ followers for visibility
- Reply to comments on your own posts within the "golden hour"

**Don't:**
- Generic praise ("Great post!")
- AI-generated comments (detectable, hurts reputation)
- Engagement bait ("Comment YES if...")
- Comment pods (penalized by algorithm)

### Daily Commenting Routine

```
Morning (10 min):
- Check notifications, reply to comments on your posts
- Comment on 3-5 posts from key accounts in your niche

Lunch (5 min):
- Quick engagement scan
- 2-3 thoughtful comments

Evening (5 min):
- Reply to new comments on your posts
- 2-3 comments on trending content in your field
```

## Building Topic Authority

LinkedIn rewards consistent expertise in specific topics.

### How to Build Authority

1. **Pick 2-3 core topics** (e.g., data visualization, Python, Spanish tech content)
2. **Post consistently** about these topics
3. **Use similar language/terms** across posts
4. **Engage with others** posting about same topics
5. **Over time**: Algorithm recognizes you as expert, boosts distribution

### Dwell Time (The Hidden Metric)

LinkedIn measures how long people read your content. Optimize for:
- Compelling hooks that earn "see more" clicks
- Formatting that keeps people reading
- Carousels that encourage swiping
- Comments that extend discussion

## Personal Brand vs Company Page

Personal profiles outperform company pages on LinkedIn.

### Strategy

- **Personal profile**: Primary content channel (higher reach)
- **Company page**: Reposts, official announcements
- **Employee advocacy**: Team members sharing company content gets 8x more engagement

For tacosdedatos (one-person operation): Focus entirely on personal profile.

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

**Algorithm Penalties:**
- External links in post body
- AI-generated content
- Engagement bait ("Like if you agree")
- Comment pods/fake engagement
- Excessive hashtags (3-5 max)

**Brand Damage:**
- Generic corporate messaging
- Over-polished content (authenticity wins)
- Ignoring comments
- Inconsistent posting
- Only promotional content
