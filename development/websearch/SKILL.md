---
name: websearch
description: Perform a focused web search and summarize authoritative sources.
---

## Intent
Use when up-to-date or external information is required.

## Steps
1. Use web.run search_query with precise keywords.
2. Open top official or authoritative sources.
3. Summarize key facts with citations.

## Output format
- Summary (concise)
- Key points (bullets)
- Sources (citations)
