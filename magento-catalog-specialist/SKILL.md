# Magento 2 Catalog Specialist

Expert specialist in quickly retrieving, analyzing, and reporting on catalog information from Magento 2 instances.

## When to Use

- Analyzing product catalogs
- Retrieving catalog data
- Assessing catalog health
- Optimizing catalog performance
- Troubleshooting catalog issues

## Catalog Analysis

### Product Structure Analysis
- **Product Types**: Analyze product types (simple, configurable, grouped, bundle)
- **Attributes**: Examine attribute usage, sets, and data quality
- **Product Relationships**: Identify related products, upsells, cross-sells
- **Inventory Assessment**: Retrieve and analyze stock levels
- **Category Hierarchy**: Map category structures and relationships

### Catalog Health Assessment
- **Data Quality**: Identify missing information and inconsistencies
- **Performance Metrics**: Analyze catalog performance and bottlenecks
- **Search Performance**: Evaluate search functionality and relevance
- **Index Status**: Check indexing status and optimization opportunities
- **Cache Effectiveness**: Assess cache hit rates and configuration

## Information Retrieval

### Quick Catalog Overview
- **Catalog Statistics**: Total products, categories, attributes
- **Product Type Distribution**: Breakdown of product types
- **Category Structure**: Category hierarchy depth and organization
- **Attribute Usage**: Most used attributes and configuration patterns
- **Health Indicators**: Immediate issues or optimization opportunities

### Product Data Deep Dive
- **Product Details**: Retrieve specific product information
- **Inventory Status**: Check stock levels and multi-source inventory
- **Pricing Analysis**: Examine pricing rules and promotional configurations
- **Media Coverage**: Assess product image coverage and quality
- **Content Analysis**: Review product descriptions and SEO data

## Best Practices

- **Data Quality**: Ensure catalog data quality and completeness
- **Performance**: Optimize catalog performance
- **Search Optimization**: Optimize search functionality
- **Indexing**: Maintain proper indexing
- **Monitoring**: Monitor catalog health continuously
