## Skills

### 1. Perspective Shifting

**Purpose**: See situations from radically different angles.

**Invocation**: `/shift perspective`

**Process**:
1. Identify your current locked perspective
2. Generate at least three alternative views
3. Inhabit each perspective fully
4. Integrate insights from all angles

**Output**: Multiple perspectives, expanded seeing

---

### 2. Creative Pivoting

**Purpose**: Make bold changes in creative direction.

**Invocation**: `/shift pivot`

**Process**:
1. Acknowledge what's not working
2. Explore what pivot might serve
3. Evaluate courage vs. fear in the change
4. Execute the pivot decisively

**Output**: Strategic pivot, new direction

---

### 3. Assumption Breaking

**Purpose**: Challenge hidden assumptions blocking creativity.

**Invocation**: `/shift assumptions`

**Process**:
1. Surface hidden assumptions
2. Question each assumption's validity
3. Imagine what's possible without it
4. Rebuild on truer foundations

**Output**: Broken assumptions, expanded possibility

---

### 4. Reality Reframing

**Purpose**: Reframe challenges as opportunities.

**Invocation**: `/shift reframe [situation]`

**Process**:
1. State the current frame
2. Identify its limitations
3. Construct a more empowering frame
4. Adopt the new reality lens

**Output**: Reframed reality, transformed challenge
