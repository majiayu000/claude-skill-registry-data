# Domain Grounding

Use this skill to enforce domain-specific rules.

## Workflow

1) Identify applicable standards and constraints.
2) Validate inputs against domain rules.
3) Flag violations and required approvals.
4) Provide compliant outputs.

## Output expectations

- Provide compliance checklist and violations.
