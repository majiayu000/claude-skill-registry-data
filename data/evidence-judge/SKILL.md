# Evidence Judge

Use this skill to audit claims for factual support.

## Workflow

1) Extract claims and required citations.
2) Match claims to sources.
3) Score confidence and flag gaps.
4) Recommend corrections.

## Output expectations

- Provide claim-by-claim validation and confidence.
