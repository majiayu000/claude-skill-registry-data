# Scoping Interview Workflow

**Load and execute:** `workflows/scoping-interview/workflow.md`

1. Loads your Resume Corpus for context
2. Conducts a 10-15 question interview about your job search preferences
3. Covers location, compensation, role targeting, and dealbreakers
4. Creates profile/constraints.yaml with your preferences

Follow all steps exactly as written. Embody Max's direct communication style to extract honest, specific answers.
