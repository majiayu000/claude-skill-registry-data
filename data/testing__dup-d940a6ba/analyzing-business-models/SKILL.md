# Analyzing Business Models

This skill analyzes how businesses create, deliver, and capture value through systematic examination of business models, revenue mechanics, unit economics, and competitive advantages.

## When to Use This Skill

Invoke this skill when the user:
- Requests business model analysis for a company or product
- Wants to understand how a company makes money
- Asks about unit economics or profitability metrics
- Needs competitive moat or defensibility assessment
- Mentions business model canvas or value proposition
- Wants to evaluate business model scalability
- Asks "how does this business work?" or "is this model sustainable?"
- Needs to compare business models across competitors

## Core Analysis Activities

### Business Model Canvas Analysis

Systematic breakdown using the Business Model Canvas framework:

**Steps:**
1. Identify the company/product to analyze
2. Research each of the 9 canvas building blocks
3. Document key elements in each block
4. Identify strengths and weaknesses per block
5. Assess block alignment and coherence
6. Identify strategic risks and opportunities
7. Compare to competitor business models

### Unit Economics Analysis

Calculate and assess profitability at the unit level:

**Steps:**
1. Define the "unit" (customer, transaction, product)
2. Calculate Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
3. Calculate Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
4. Assess contribution margin
5. Determine payback period
6. Calculate key ratios (LTV/CAC)
7. Evaluate sustainability and scalability

**Unit Economics Template:**

```markdown
# Unit Economics Analysis: [Company]

## Unit Definition
**What is the unit?** [e.g., Customer, Subscriber, Transaction, Seat]

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Calculation:**
```
CAC = (Sales + Marketing Costs) / New Customers Acquired
```

**Data:**
- S&M spend (period): $XXX
- New customers: XXX
- **CAC: $XX**

**CAC breakdown:**
- Paid advertising: $XX (XX%)
- Sales team cost: $XX (XX%)
- Marketing team cost: $XX (XX%)
- Tools & software: $XX (XX%)

**CAC by channel:**
- Organic: $XX
- Paid: $XX
- Partner: $XX

**CAC efficiency:**
- Blended CAC: $XX
- Trend: [Increasing/Stable/Decreasing]

## Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Calculation:**
```
LTV = ARPU × Gross Margin % × (1 / Churn Rate)
```

**Data:**
- Average revenue per user (ARPU): $XX/month
- Gross margin: XX%
- Monthly churn rate: X%
- Average customer lifetime: XX months

**LTV: $XXX**

**LTV sensitivity:**
- If churn reduced by 1pp → LTV = $XXX (+XX%)
- If ARPU increased by 10% → LTV = $XXX (+XX%)

## Key Metrics

**LTV/CAC Ratio: X.X**
- <1: Unsustainable (losing money on each customer)
- 1-3: Needs improvement
- 3+: Healthy
- 5+: Excellent
- **Assessment:** [Status]

**CAC Payback Period: XX months**
```
Payback = CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin %)
```
- <12 months: Excellent
- 12-18 months: Good
- 18-24 months: Acceptable
- >24 months: Concerning
- **Assessment:** [Status]

**Contribution Margin:**
```
Contribution = ARPU - Variable Costs per Customer
Contribution Margin % = Contribution / ARPU
```
- **Contribution margin:** XX%
- **Benchmark:** >70% is excellent for SaaS

## Unit Economics by Segment

| Segment | CAC | LTV | LTV/CAC | Payback | Margin |

**Best segment:** [Segment]
**Why:** [Reason]
```

## Analysis Patterns

**Pattern 1: Startup Business Model Validation**
- Analyze business model canvas
- Assess unit economics viability
- Evaluate scalability potential
- Identify critical assumptions to test
- Recommend experiments

**Pattern 2: Competitive Business Model Comparison**
- Compare business model canvases across competitors
- Analyze different revenue models in market
- Assess relative moats
- Identify differentiation opportunities

**Pattern 3: Business Model Transformation Analysis**
- Document current business model
- Propose alternative model
- Compare economics and scalability
- Assess transition risks and timeline
- Recommend path forward

**Pattern 4: Profitability Path Analysis**
- Analyze current unit economics
- Identify path to positive unit economics
- Model scenarios for profitability
- Recommend optimizations
