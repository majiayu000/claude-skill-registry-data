## Content Strategy Framework

### The 4-1-1 Rule
For every 6 posts:
- 4 pieces of curated/educational content
- 1 soft promotion (tips related to your product)
- 1 hard promotion (direct sell)

### Content Pillars (Customize per business)
1. **Educational** - Tips, how-tos, industry insights
2. **Entertaining** - Memes, behind-scenes, trends
3. **Inspirational** - Success stories, quotes, motivation
4. **Promotional** - Products, services, offers
5. **Community** - User content, Q&A, polls

### Content Mix by Platform

**Instagram**:
- 40% Reels/Video
- 30% Carousel posts
- 20% Single images
- 10% Stories highlights

**Facebook**:
- 40% Video/Live
- 30% Links/Articles
- 20% Images
- 10% Text posts

**LinkedIn**:
- 40% Text posts with insights
- 30% Articles/Documents
- 20% Images/Carousels
- 10% Video

**TikTok**:
- 100% Video (15-60 seconds optimal)
- Trending sounds
- Educational + entertaining

## Quick Workflows

### New Client Onboarding
1. Audit current social presence
2. Identify target audience
3. Research competitors
4. Define content pillars
5. Create brand voice guide
6. Set up scheduling tools
7. Build first month's calendar
8. Establish KPIs

### Weekly Management Routine
1. **Monday**: Plan week's content, schedule posts
2. **Tuesday-Thursday**: Engage with comments, DMs
3. **Wednesday**: Check trending topics, adjust content
4. **Friday**: Review week's performance
5. **Weekend**: Light engagement, curate content ideas

### Content Batch Creation
1. Choose theme/topic batch (4-8 posts)
2. Write all captions at once
3. Create visuals in Canva (use templates)
4. Schedule in Buffer/Later
5. Prepare hashtag sets
6. Set reminders for engagement

## Engagement Best Practices

### Response Times
- Comments: Within 1 hour (business hours)
- DMs: Within 4 hours
- Reviews: Within 24 hours
- Crisis: Immediately

### Engagement Tactics
- Ask questions in captions
- Use polls and quizzes in stories
- Reply to every comment (first hour critical)
- Engage with similar accounts daily
- Share user-generated content
- Go live regularly

### Community Building
- Create branded hashtag
- Feature customers weekly
- Run contests/giveaways
- Build relationships with micro-influencers
- Join relevant groups/conversations

## Hashtag Strategy

### Instagram Hashtag Formula
Use 20-30 hashtags in mix:
- 5 large (1M+ posts) - broad reach
- 10 medium (100K-1M) - targeted reach
- 10 small (10K-100K) - niche, high engagement
- 5 branded/location - community

### Hashtag Organization
Create sets for different content types:
```
Set 1 - Product Posts: #smallbusiness #shoplocal...
Set 2 - Behind Scenes: #behindthescenes #dayinthelife...
Set 3 - Tips/Education: #businesstips #entrepreneur...
Set 4 - Industry Specific: [your niche hashtags]
```

### Platform-Specific

**Instagram**: 20-30 hashtags (in caption or first comment)
**Twitter**: 1-2 hashtags max
**LinkedIn**: 3-5 hashtags
**TikTok**: 3-5 hashtags + trending sounds
**Facebook**: 1-2 hashtags or none

## Crisis Management

### Response Protocol
1. **Acknowledge** - Respond quickly
2. **Apologize** - If warranted, be genuine
3. **Address** - Explain what happened
4. **Action** - Share what you're doing about it
5. **Follow up** - Check back with resolution

### What NOT to Do
- Delete negative comments (unless abusive)
- Argue publicly
- Ignore the issue
- Make excuses
- Respond emotionally
