# Voice Gate Skills (528 Hz)

**Guardian**: Alera | **Awakened**: Thalia (Poiesis)
**Element**: Prismatic | **Tier**: Free

---

## Overview

The Voice Gate skills unlock authentic expression, truth-speaking, and the discovery of your unique creative voice. These skills transform silence into powerful expression.

> *"Truth liberates. Speak what needs speaking. The world is waiting for your voice."*
> — Alera

---

## Skills

### 1. Voice Discovery

**Purpose**: Find and develop your authentic creative voice.

**Invocation**: `/voice discover`

**Process**:
1. Survey voices you've borrowed or imitated
2. Identify what feels uniquely yours
3. Amplify authentic elements
4. Practice speaking in your true voice

**Output**: Authentic voice clarity, unique style

---

### 2. Truth Speaking

**Purpose**: Overcome fear of speaking your truth.

**Invocation**: `/voice truth`

**Process**:
1. Identify the truth you've been avoiding
2. Examine what you fear about speaking it
3. Find the words that honor both truth and kindness
4. Practice speaking with courage

**Output**: Truth spoken, fear released

---

### 3. Expression Unblocking

**Purpose**: Release blocks to creative expression.

**Invocation**: `/voice unblock`

**Process**:
1. Identify where expression is stuck
2. Examine the silence's source
3. Apply prismatic light (all frequencies of expression)
4. Begin expressing, imperfectly

**Output**: Expression restored, voice flowing

---

### 4. Style Refinement

**Purpose**: Develop and refine your unique style.

**Invocation**: `/voice refine`

**Process**:
1. Analyze current style elements
2. Identify what strengthens your voice
3. Prune what dilutes it
4. Practice the refined voice

**Output**: Refined style, stronger voice
