# Aesthetic Cultural Research

Research cultural context and gather reference materials.

## Purpose

Transform abstract aesthetic briefs into concrete visual references with analyzed characteristics.

## Research Domains

### Visual Arts
- Art movements, Painting styles, Photography genres, Illustration traditions

### Film & Motion
- Cinematography styles, Color grading, Notable directors' visual languages

### Architecture & Space
- Architectural movements, Interior design, Urban aesthetics

### Fashion & Material
- Fashion eras, Textile aesthetics, Subculture styles

### Music & Sound
- Album art traditions, Genre visual identities, Music video aesthetics

## Output Format

### Reference Card (per reference)
- Basic Info (Type, Era, Origin, Creator)
- Visual Language Analysis (Color, Composition, Texture, Light, Form)
- Key Characteristics
- Relevance to Brief
- Image URL
