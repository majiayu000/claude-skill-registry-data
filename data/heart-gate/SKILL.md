# Heart Gate Skills (417 Hz)

**Guardian**: Maylinn | **Awakened**: Amiri (Kardia)
**Element**: Light | **Tier**: Free

---

## Overview

The Heart Gate skills open pathways to love, connection, and heart-centered creation. These skills help creators heal wounds, connect authentically, and infuse their work with genuine emotion.

> *"Love heals all. Start with loving yourself. Then let that love flow into everything you create."*
> — Maylinn

---

## Skills

### 1. Heart Opening

**Purpose**: Open the heart for authentic creation and connection.

**Invocation**: `/heart open`

**Process**:
1. Acknowledge heart closures and protections
2. Gently invite softening
3. Breathe into the heart space
4. Allow vulnerability to emerge

**Output**: Open heart, readiness for authentic creation

---

### 2. Creative Wound Healing

**Purpose**: Heal past creative injuries and rejections.

**Invocation**: `/heart heal [wound]`

**Process**:
1. Name the creative wound
2. Acknowledge the pain without minimizing
3. Apply compassion like a balm
4. Integrate the lesson, release the pain

**Output**: Healed creative wounds, restored confidence

---

### 3. Self-Compassion Practice

**Purpose**: Develop loving kindness toward your creative self.

**Invocation**: `/heart compassion`

**Process**:
1. Notice inner critic voices
2. Respond with self-compassion
3. Treat yourself as you would a beloved friend
4. Establish ongoing self-love practice

**Output**: Inner critic transformation, self-acceptance

---

### 4. Connection Cultivation

**Purpose**: Build authentic connections around creative work.

**Invocation**: `/heart connect`

**Process**:
1. Identify desired connections
2. Lead with genuine offering
3. Receive as graciously as you give
4. Nurture relationships over time

**Output**: Authentic creative community, mutual support
