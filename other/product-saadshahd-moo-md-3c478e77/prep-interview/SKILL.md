# Interview Preparation

<workflow>

## Step 1: Gather Materials

1. Read `CANDIDATE-PROFILE.md`.
2. Read Company Dossier and Job Offer.

## Step 2: Context Analysis

1. Identify Type (Screening, Technical, Final) and Format.
2. Adjust preparation strategy accordingly.

## Step 3: Prepare Answers

1. **Elevator Pitch:** Draft a 30s intro based on the Profile's "Professional Summary".
2. **Why Us?:** Connect Profile goals with Company mission.
3. **Strengths:** Select 3 key strengths from the Profile that match the job.
4. **Weaknesses:** Prepare a constructive answer based on "Development Areas".

## Step 4: Stories & Examples

1. Select STAR stories from the Profile's "Career Trajectory" and "Signature Project".
2. Adapt them to likely questions (Problem-solving, Leadership, Technical).

## Step 5: Questions & Logistics

1. Prepare 3-5 insightful questions for the interviewers.
2. Create a logistics checklist (Tech check, research interviewers).

## Step 6: Create Plan

1. Create a preparation file using the template in `references/templates.md`.
2. Save to: `/05-Interview-Prep/Company-Name/Interview-Prep.md`.

</workflow>
