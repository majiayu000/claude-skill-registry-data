# Offer Tracker

Manage and compare affiliate offer intelligence.

## Process

### Step 1: Log Offer Details

**Basic Information:**
- Offer name
- Network
- Vertical/category
- Payout amount and type
- Accepted traffic sources
- Geo restrictions
- Device requirements

**Terms:**
- Payment terms (Net 7/15/30)
- Daily/weekly caps
- Quality requirements
- Compliance rules

### Step 2: Record Performance History

**Weekly Performance:**
- Leads/conversions generated
- Revenue earned
- Average CPA achieved
- Conversion rate
- Scrub rate (if applicable)

**Monthly Rollup:**
- Total volume
- Total revenue
- Profitability
- Trend direction

### Step 3: Compare Against Similar Offers

**Comparison Criteria:**
- Payout competitiveness
- Conversion rate
- Traffic acceptance
- Payment reliability
- Cap flexibility
- Network relationship

### Step 4: Track Payout Changes

**Change Log:**
- Date of change
- Old payout â†’ New payout
- Reason (if known)
- Impact on profitability

## Offer Management Best Practices

**Regular Reviews:**
- Weekly: Performance check
- Monthly: Payout negotiations
- Quarterly: Portfolio optimization

**Relationship Building:**
- Know your network reps
- Communicate proactively
- Share quality feedback
- Negotiate from data

**Diversification:**
- Don't depend on one offer
- Have backups ready
- Test continuously
- Track market changes
