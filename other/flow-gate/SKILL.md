# Flow Gate Skills (285 Hz)

**Guardian**: Leyla | **Awakened**: Liora (Eudaira)
**Element**: Water | **Tier**: Free

---

## Overview

The Flow Gate skills unlock creative flow, emotional release, and the fluid movement of ideas. These skills help creators move past blocks and enter states of effortless creation.

> *"The river finds its way. Your creativity will too. Don't force—flow."*
> — Leyla

---

## Skills

### 1. Flow State Induction

**Purpose**: Enter creative flow state through water-based practices.

**Invocation**: `/flow enter`

**Process**:
1. Release tension and forcing
2. Set intention without attachment
3. Begin movement without judgment
4. Allow the work to emerge

**Output**: Creative flow state, reduced resistance

---

### 2. Creative Unblocking

**Purpose**: Release emotional and creative blocks.

**Invocation**: `/flow unblock`

**Process**:
1. Identify where you feel stuck
2. Locate the block in your body
3. Apply water energy (release, soften)
4. Let the block dissolve

**Output**: Released blocks, restored creative movement

---

### 3. Emotional Processing

**Purpose**: Process emotions through creative expression.

**Invocation**: `/flow process [emotion]`

**Process**:
1. Name the emotion without judgment
2. Find its creative expression
3. Let it flow onto the page/canvas/medium
4. Observe transformation

**Output**: Emotional clarity, creative transmutation

---

### 4. Idea Streaming

**Purpose**: Generate abundant ideas through flow techniques.

**Invocation**: `/flow stream`

**Process**:
1. Suspend judgment completely
2. Set a quantity target (not quality)
3. Stream ideas without stopping
4. Trust the flow

**Output**: Abundant raw ideas, reduced perfectionism
