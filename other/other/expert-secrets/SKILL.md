# Expert Secrets

> Build a mass movement around your expertise using Russell Brunson's proven framework—position yourself as the charismatic leader, offer a new opportunity, and create a future-based cause that turns followers into paying customers.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:

- **Position yourself as an expert** in your field
- **Create an online course or coaching program** based on your knowledge
- **Build a tribe** of loyal followers around your message
- **Design a webinar** that converts attendees into buyers
- **Craft your origin story** and transformation narrative
- **Package your expertise** into sellable products
- **Create compelling offers** using the Stack Slide technique
- **Overcome objections** by breaking false belief patterns

This skill is particularly valuable for:
- Coaches, consultants, and course creators
- Anyone monetizing their expertise online
- Entrepreneurs launching info products
- Thought leaders building personal brands
- Anyone who wants to turn knowledge into income

## Instructions

### Part 1: Creating Your Mass Movement

Every mass movement requires three elements:

| Element | Purpose | Question to Answer |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| **Charismatic Leader** | Someone to follow | Who are you as a guide? |
| **New Opportunity** | A vehicle for change | What transformation do you offer? |
| **Future-Based Cause** | A vision to unite around | Where are you leading them? |

---

### Element 1: The Charismatic Leader

#### Choose Your Attractive Character Type

**The Leader**
- Lead by example
- Share your experiences and expertise
- Demonstrate success others can emulate
- Best when: You've achieved remarkable results

**The Adventurer/Crusader**
- Take followers on a journey
- Explore new grounds and break barriers
- Not afraid of risks
- Best when: You're discovering alongside your audience

**The Reporter/Relater**
- Bridge between experts and audience
- Gather, organize, and share information
- Bring insights from top experts
- Best when: You're newer but passionate about learning

#### The Five Phases to Becoming an Expert

| Phase | Action | Key Activities |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| **1. The Dreamer** | Feel the calling | Recognize your spark and desire to help |
| **2. The Reporter** | Learn everything | Interview experts, attend events, consume content |
| **3. Framework Builder** | Create your method | Synthesize learnings into proprietary frameworks |
| **4. Work for Free** | Test on others | Find beta group, gather testimonials |
| **5. The Expert** | Publish and scale | Daily content, documented journey, refined material |

**The Publishing Commitment:**
> "If you can publish every day for a year, you'll never have to worry about money problems again."

---

### Element 2: The New Opportunity

#### Why New Opportunity Beats Improvement

People don't want to be told their current approach is wrong (improvement offer). They want a completely new vehicle for achieving their desires (new opportunity).

| Improvement Offer | New Opportunity |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| "Fix your diet" | "Try intermittent fasting" |
| "Work harder at sales" | "Use this new closing system" |
| "Improve your ads" | "Build funnels instead" |
| Implies past failure | Fresh start, no baggage |

#### The Status Secret

> "Almost every decision you've ever made was based on this one subconscious question: Is this thing I'm considering going to increase my status or decrease it?"

**What stops action?** Fear of decreased status.

**Your job:** Load up the elevated-status side and minimize risk through:
- Money-back guarantees
- Risk reversals
- Done-for-you options
- Social proof

#### Finding Your New Opportunity

**Step 1:** Identify the result your dream customers want

**Step 2:** Identify the vehicle they're currently using

**Step 3:** Create your opportunity switch (new vehicle)

**Step 4:** Stack additional value on the new opportunity

### Part 2: Creating Belief

#### The Big Domino

Find the ONE belief that, if they believe it, makes buying inevitable.

**Template:**
"If I can make people believe that **[my new opportunity]** is the key to **[the result they desire most]** and is only available through **[my specific vehicle/frameworks]**, then all other objections and concerns become irrelevant and they have to give me money."

**Example:**
"If I can make people believe that **sales funnels** are the key to **growing their online business** and are only available through **ClickFunnels**, then all other objections become irrelevant."

#### The Epiphany Bridge

A storytelling formula that guides your audience to their own "aha" moment.

**The Epiphany Bridge Script:**

1. **The Backstory** - Context and setup
2. **The Wall** - The problem you encountered (similar to theirs)
3. **The Epiphany** - The realization that changed everything
4. **The Transformation** - The result after the breakthrough

**Key Insight:** Don't just tell them what to believe—take them on a journey so they arrive at the belief themselves.

#### Breaking False Belief Patterns

Three types of false beliefs to overcome:

| Belief Type | What It Is | Example Objection |
|-------------|------------|-------------------|
| **Vehicle** | About your opportunity | "Funnels don't work in my industry" |
| **Internal** | About themselves | "I'm not tech-savvy enough" |
| **External** | About outside factors | "I don't have the time/money" |

**For each false belief, you need:**
- A story that breaks the old belief
- A new belief to replace it

### Part 3: The Perfect Webinar

#### Structure Overview

**Section 1: The Intro (5-10 minutes)**
- Hook them with a compelling promise
- Establish credibility
- Preview the offer (create curiosity)

**Section 2: The Content (45-60 minutes)**
- Secret #1: The One Thing (breaks Vehicle belief)
- Secret #2: The Hidden Benefit (breaks Internal belief)
- Secret #3: The New Opportunity (breaks External belief)

**Section 3: The Stack & Close (20-30 minutes)**
- Present the Stack Slide
- Use Trial Closes throughout
- Handle objections
- Create urgency
- Call to action

#### The Three Secrets Framework

| Secret | Purpose | Belief It Breaks |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| **Secret #1: The One Thing** | Share unique insight that changes their thinking | Vehicle (about the opportunity) |
| **Secret #2: The Hidden Benefit** | Reveal unexpected advantage | Internal (about themselves) |
| **Secret #3: The New Opportunity** | Present your complete system | External (about circumstances) |

Each secret follows the Epiphany Bridge structure.

#### Trial Closes

Micro-commitments throughout your presentation:

**When to use:**
- After each teaching point: "Can you see why this is important?"
- Before the pitch: "Can you see how this could work for you?"
- During the stack: "Can you see how this bonus alone is worth the price?"
- Before closing: "Can you imagine the impact this would have?"

**Purpose:** Create a pattern of "yes" so the final yes (buying) feels natural.

### Part 4: The Stack Slide

#### Building Your Stack

**Step 1:** Define your core offer (the main thing they're buying)

**Step 2:** Add supporting elements:
- Bonus #1: Addresses Vehicle belief
- Bonus #2: Addresses Internal belief
- Bonus #3: Addresses External belief
- Fast-action bonuses
- Guarantees

**Step 3:** Assign value to each element

**Step 4:** Present each item, building perceived value

**Step 5:** Total the stack value (should be 10x the price)

**Step 6:** Reveal the actual price

#### Value Presentation Template

```
Here's everything you're getting:

[Core Offer] .......................... $X,XXX value
[Bonus #1: Addresses concern] ......... $XXX value
[Bonus #2: Saves time/effort] ......... $XXX value
[Bonus #3: Additional resource] ....... $XXX value
[Fast-action bonus] ................... $XXX value

Total Value: $XX,XXX

But you're not paying $XX,XXX...
You're not even paying $X,XXX...

Today, your investment is just $XXX
```
