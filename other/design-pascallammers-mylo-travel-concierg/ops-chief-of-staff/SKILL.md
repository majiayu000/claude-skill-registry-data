# Ops Chief of Staff

Use this skill to execute operational workflows with compliance awareness.

## Workflow

1) Identify the operational domain (compliance, HR, finance).
2) Gather required inputs and verify permissions.
3) Execute the workflow with audit logging.
4) Report outcomes and outstanding approvals.

## Output expectations

- Provide a clear checklist and audit log summary.
