# AdaptiveX Brand Styling

## Overview

To access AdaptiveX's official brand identity and style resources, use this skill. AdaptiveX is a professional training and consulting firm specializing in organizational agility, innovation, and AI integration at enterprise scale.

**Keywords**: branding, corporate identity, visual identity, post-processing, styling, brand colors, typography, AdaptiveX brand, visual formatting, visual design, agility training, innovation consulting

## Brand Guidelines

### Colors

**Main Colors:**

- AX Purple: `#6B3FA0` - Primary brand color (verify exact hex with brand assets)
- AX Yellow: `#F9C80E` - Secondary accent color (verify exact hex with brand assets)

**Supporting Colors:**

- Dark Gray: `#2C2C2C` - Primary text and dark backgrounds
- Light Gray: `#F5F5F5` - Light backgrounds
- Mid Gray: `#808080` - Secondary elements
- White: `#FFFFFF` - Clean backgrounds and reversed text

**Usage Guidelines:**
- AX Purple: Primary brand color for headers, key elements, and brand presence
- AX Yellow: Accent color for highlights, calls-to-action, and energy points
- Use high contrast combinations for accessibility (purple on white, white on purple)
- Avoid using yellow on white backgrounds (insufficient contrast)

### Typography

- **Primary Font**: Averta (geometric sans-serif)
  - Headings: Averta Bold or Semibold
  - Subheadings: Averta Medium
  - Body Text: Averta Regular
  - Captions: Averta Light

- **Fallback Fonts**: 
  - Primary fallback: Arial
  - Secondary fallback: Helvetica, sans-serif

**Note**: Averta should be licensed and installed in your environment. It's a versatile geometric sans-serif designed for cross-media use with 8 weights plus italics.

## Features

### Typography Hierarchy

- **Headings (H1)**: Averta Bold, 32-40pt, AX Purple
- **Headings (H2)**: Averta Semibold, 24-28pt, AX Purple or Dark Gray
- **Headings (H3)**: Averta Medium, 18-20pt, Dark Gray
- **Body Text**: Averta Regular, 12-14pt, Dark Gray
- **Emphasis**: Averta Semibold or Medium for inline emphasis
- **Captions**: Averta Light, 10-11pt, Mid Gray

### Color Application Strategy

**For Presentations:**
- Title slides: AX Purple background with white text
- Content slides: White background with AX Purple headers
- Accent elements: AX Yellow for highlights and key takeaways
- Section dividers: Full AX Purple or gradient purple-to-dark

**For Documents:**
- Headers: AX Purple
- Body: Dark Gray on white
- Callout boxes: Light gray background with AX Purple border
- Highlights: AX Yellow background (with sufficient text contrast)

**For Digital Materials:**
- Primary CTAs: AX Yellow background with dark text
- Secondary CTAs: AX Purple background with white text
- Links: AX Purple (hover: darker purple)
- Navigation: White on AX Purple or AX Purple on white

### Visual Elements

**When using shapes and graphics:**
- Primary shapes: AX Purple fill
- Accent shapes: AX Yellow fill
- Supporting elements: Grayscale palette
- Icons: Line icons in AX Purple or solid fills in brand colors
- Avoid mixing too many accent colors - stick to purple and yellow as primary visual anchors

## Technical Details

### Font Management

- **Averta Family**: Professional geometric sans-serif by Kostas Bartsokas (Fontfabric)
- **Weights Available**: 8 weights from Thin to Black plus matching italics
- **Character Sets**: Supports Latin, Cyrillic, and Greek
- **Web Use**: Requires web font license from Fontfabric
- **Desktop Use**: Requires desktop license for design applications

**Installation Notes:**
- For best results, install the complete Averta family
- Fallback chain: Averta, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif
- Web fonts should be loaded via @font-face with proper licensing

### Color Technical Specifications

**For Digital Use (RGB):**
- AX Purple: RGB(107, 63, 160) - *verify with official brand assets*
- AX Yellow: RGB(249, 200, 14) - *verify with official brand assets*
- Dark Gray: RGB(44, 44, 44)
- Mid Gray: RGB(128, 128, 128)
- Light Gray: RGB(245, 245, 245)

**For Print Use (CMYK):**
- AX Purple: *Requires verification with brand assets*
- AX Yellow: *Requires verification with brand assets*
- Dark Gray: C0 M0 Y0 K83
- Mid Gray: C0 M0 Y0 K50
- Light Gray: C0 M0 Y0 K4

**Accessibility:**
- Purple on white: WCAG AA compliant
- White on purple: WCAG AA compliant
- Yellow on white: FAILS - never use this combination
- Dark text on yellow: Verify contrast ratio (aim for 4.5:1 minimum)

### Application Guidelines

**Professional Development Materials:**
- Maintain clean, uncluttered layouts
- Use purple strategically - not overwhelming
- Yellow sparingly for emphasis only
- Generous white space reflects professional expertise

**Training Presentations:**
- Title slide: Full purple background
- Agenda/overview: White with purple headers
- Content: Minimalist with strategic color use
- Key takeaways: Yellow accent boxes
- Q&A/closing: Return to purple branding

**Workshop Materials:**
- Handouts: Conservative use of color (print-friendly)
- Workbooks: Purple section headers, yellow highlights for important notes
- Digital templates: Full color with interactive elements in brand colors
